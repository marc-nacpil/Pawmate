Furry Pals

Birman Cat

A strikingly beautiful breed with stunning blue eyes and a gentle, affectionate personality, often described as a "people-oriented" cat.

Siamese Cat

An elegant, vocal breed with striking blue eyes and color points, known for its affectionate nature.

Rag Doll Cat

A large, affectionate breed that goes limp when picked up, known for its striking blue eyes and friendly, easygoing temperament.

Mynx Cat

A unique breed known for its taillessness and playful nature, with a strong bond to its family and a variety of coat colors and patterns.

Persian Cat

A long-haired breed with a flat face and a calm demeanor, cherished for its luxurious coat and loving companionship.

Orange Cat

Friendly and playful, these ginger cats are recognized for their warm, affectionate personalities and come in various breeds.
