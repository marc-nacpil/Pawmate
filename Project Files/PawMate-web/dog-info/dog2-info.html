The Boston Terrier, affectionately known as the "American Gentleman," is a small breed with distinctive tuxedo-like markings, a short snout, and large expressive eyes. Friendly, smart, and full of energy, this breed is a great companion for families, singles, and seniors alike. Boston Terriers are known for their playful nature and thrive on human interaction, making them excellent pets for those who enjoy spending time with their dogs. While lively, they are not overly demanding and are content with moderate daily exercise. Their compact size makes them ideal for apartment living, but they also enjoy outdoor play sessions. Boston Terriers are typically good with children and other pets when properly socialized. Training them is relatively easy due to their eagerness to please, though they can have a stubborn streak. They have minimal grooming needs but may require some care during hot weather due to their short muzzle.
