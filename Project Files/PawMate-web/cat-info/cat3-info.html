The Ragdoll cat is a large, affectionate breed known for its striking blue eyes and luxurious, semi-long fur. Originating in the United States, Ragdolls are famous for their gentle and laid-back temperament, often described as "puppy-like" due to their tendency to follow their owners around the house. They are incredibly friendly and sociable, enjoying the company of humans and other pets, making them excellent family companions. Ragdolls are known for their unique behavior of going limp when picked up, hence the name "Ragdoll." They are playful yet calm, requiring moderate exercise and mental stimulation. Their grooming needs are manageable, as regular brushing helps prevent matting of their soft fur. Ragdolls thrive in homes where they receive plenty of love and attention, and their affectionate nature ensures they quickly become beloved members of any family. With their sweet personalities and gentle demeanor, Ragdolls are a joy to have around.
